Y2K Guidebook

A comprehensive guidebook exploring the vibrant world of Y2K aesthetics. This project delves into the fashion, design, movies, music, and games that defined the era. Featuring a bold colour palette, custom icons, and a layout that balances information with visual appeal, the book captures the essence of Y2K culture. Part of a larger series on neo-aesthetics, this guidebook aims to educate and inspire readers about this influential period in design history.

The book's cover features holographic foil printing, adding a striking, iridescent effect that perfectly captures the futuristic and bold aesthetic of the Y2K era.

This project marked my first venture into book design, significantly enhancing my skills in typography, layout design, and Adobe InDesign, while deepening my understanding of creating cohesive and visually engaging publications.
